Edible Blob - The Future of water & Beverages

We are on a mission to make our planet earth a better place to live by shifting to sustainable method of consuming water & beverages.

The main issue being addressed here is the overconsumption of plastic and glass even after knowing their hazardous side effects being caused both to nature and the living sections of the environment. Plastic and glass create immense damage to the environment which is beyond our imagination. They are non-biodegradable materials, they can never decompose. Burning of plastic releases toxic gases and glass being a brittle material when broken down releases chemicals like silicon, lead, arsenic, and cadmium into the environment. Plastic affects wildlife especially marine life leading to suffocation, entanglement, and ingestion. Even after knowledge gained about all of these issues addressed above why is consumption increasing and not declining? The simple answer is that there is no mindful consumption pattern being followed for sustainable development. These elements like plastic and glass are readily available, and cost-effective due to which the side effects rendered by these harmful elements are not valued more.
